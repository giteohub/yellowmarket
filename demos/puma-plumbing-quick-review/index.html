Opportunities → YellowMarket products

Build the lead system around the opportunity.

Each pain point maps to a concrete YellowMarket product, so the review sells outcomes — not just a prettier page.

1. Google Maps visibility

Friction: local homeowners often choose from Maps before reading the full site.
Product: GBP cleanup, service categories, photo/review prompts, and local landing page alignment.

2. Missed-call receptionist

Friction: urgent plumbing/HVAC calls can arrive while the owner is on a job.
Product: AI receptionist captures emergency vs estimate, property details, and sends Puma a structured SMS.

3. Service-specific quote intake

Friction: plumbing repair, HVAC repair, install, and maintenance should not use the same vague form.
Product: guided quote form that routes by service type and urgency.

4. Maintenance follow-up workflow

Friction: tune-ups and recurring HVAC work are easy to lose after first contact.
Product: reminder + calendar workflow for tune-ups, callbacks, and quote follow-up.
