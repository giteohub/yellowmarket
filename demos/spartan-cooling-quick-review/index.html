  • Scheduling must protect the crew calendar.
Opportunities → YellowMarket products

Build the lead system around the opportunity.

Each opportunity maps to a concrete YellowMarket product, so the review sells outcomes — not just a prettier page.

1. Emergency call routing

Friction: hot-weather repair leads are time-sensitive.
Product: AI receptionist or call routing that prioritizes no-cooling/emergency cases and texts the job summary.

2. Google Maps visibility

Friction: “AC repair near me” decisions happen fast in Maps.
Product: GBP cleanup, service categories, emergency/tune-up/replacement posts, review prompts.

3. Guided HVAC estimate intake

Friction: replacement/tune-up/commercial jobs need different details.
Product: form flow by service type: repair, install, tune-up, refrigeration, with preferred windows.

4. Calendar + dispatch control

Friction: fully automatic booking needs guardrails to protect technician availability.
Product: preferred time windows, urgency tags, and internal confirmation before final booking.
