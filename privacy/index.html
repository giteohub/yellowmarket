Simple version: We collect only what we need to respond to your audit request and deliver services. We never sell your data. We never share your phone number with third parties for marketing. You can ask us to delete your information anytime.

YellowMarket may use privacy-conscious analytics or advertising pixels to understand visits, improve pages, and measure campaign performance. If advertising tools are added, they may collect browser, device, page visit, and interaction data according to their own policies.

Sharing

YellowMarket does not sell submitted contact information. Information may be shared with service providers only when needed for website hosting, form processing, email delivery, phone/SMS, analytics, or approved client work.

Retention

We keep business inquiries and project information for as long as needed to respond, deliver services, maintain records, and improve operations. You may request deletion of your contact information unless retention is needed for legitimate business or legal reasons.

Calls and text communications

If you provide a phone number and consent to be contacted about your audit, YellowMarket may call or text you about that request or services you approve. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out of texts. Consent is not a condition of purchase and is not required to receive your free audit. We never share your phone number with third parties for marketing.

Your choices

You can ask to access, correct, or delete information you submitted by emailing contact@yellowmarket.store.

Our services are for business owners and professionals. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 13. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, contact contact@yellowmarket.store.
