Privacy Policy

This policy explains how YellowMarket LLC collects and uses information submitted through yellowmarket.store.

We use submitted information to review your business presence, prepare a practical audit, reply to your request, provide quotes, and deliver services you approve.

Website forms may be processed by FormSubmit or similar third-party form tools and delivered to YellowMarket by email. Do not submit sensitive personal, payment, health, legal, or confidential business data through the public form.

Analytics and advertising

YellowMarket may use privacy-conscious analytics or advertising pixels to understand visits, improve pages, and measure campaign performance. If advertising tools are added, they may collect browser, device, page visit, and interaction data according to their own policies.

Sharing

YellowMarket does not sell submitted contact information. Information may be shared with service providers only when needed for website hosting, form processing, email delivery, phone/SMS, analytics, or approved client work.

Retention

We keep business inquiries and project information for as long as needed to respond, deliver services, maintain records, and improve operations. You may request deletion of your contact information unless retention is needed for legitimate business or legal reasons.

Your choices

You can ask to access, correct, or delete information you submitted by emailing contact@yellowmarket.store.
