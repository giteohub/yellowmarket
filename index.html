The Revenue Leak Problem

Your website and Google presence are working against you — and you're paying for it every month.

Here's what happens to a typical local business lead:

1

A potential customer finds you on Google

Your GBP listing is incomplete, has 3 old reviews, and no service pages rank for what they searched. They move to the next result.

2
